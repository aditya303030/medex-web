About
What is our motive?
Medex is a project made help the poor by donating unused and unexpired medicines. Many poor people living in rural areas have less or no access to medicines and we can resolve this issue by donating unused and unexpired medicine.
Who made this website?
My name is Aditya and I am in grade 12. I am currently studying at Delhi Public School - Bangalore East. This is a project I started so that we can help the poor have regular access to commonly used medicines.
