    Medex is a platform created to donate unused and unexpired medicine to the unprivileged people of India. Hospitals are beyond the reach of several people in India due to financial reasons and the least they should be having is a regular supply of commonly used medicines for basic treatements at home. An estimated 469 million people in India do not have regular access to essential medicines, according to the WHO.

    Since the poor can't afford expensive medicines, a donation from the priviledged people who can actually afford it would be of great help.
